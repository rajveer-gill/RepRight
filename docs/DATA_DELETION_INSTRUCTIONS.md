# Data Deletion Instructions for RepRight

**Last Updated:** November 11, 2025

## How to Delete Your Data

RepRight stores all your data locally on your device. You have complete control over your data and can delete it at any time using the methods below.

## Method 1: Delete All Data Through the App (Recommended)

1. Open the RepRight app on your iPhone or iPad
2. Navigate to the **Profile** tab (bottom navigation)
3. Scroll down to find **"Delete All Data"** or **"Reset App"** option
4. Tap the option and confirm your choice
5. All your data will be permanently deleted, including:
   - Your user profile (name, age, fitness level, goals, etc.)
   - All workout plans
   - Workout history and progress
   - Streak counts
   - Saved videos (if any)
   - All app settings

**Note:** This action cannot be undone. You will need to complete the onboarding process again if you want to use the app.

## Method 2: Delete Data by Uninstalling the App

1. On your iPhone or iPad, find the RepRight app icon
2. Press and hold the app icon
3. Tap **"Remove App"** or the **"X"** button
4. Select **"Delete App"** to confirm
5. All app data stored on your device will be permanently deleted

**Note:** Uninstalling the app removes all data, including your profile, workout plans, and progress.

## What Data is Deleted?

When you delete your data, the following information is permanently removed from your device:

- **Profile Information**: Name, age, fitness level, goals, workout preferences, available equipment, health restrictions
- **Workout Plans**: All generated and saved workout plans
- **Workout History**: Completed workouts, exercise progress, streak counts
- **Form Analysis Data**: Any saved form analysis results
- **Videos**: Any workout videos stored locally (if applicable)
- **App Settings**: All app preferences and configurations

## Data Stored with Third-Party Services

RepRight uses the following third-party services:

### OpenAI API
- **Data Sent**: Your profile information and video frames (for form analysis)
- **Data Retention**: We do not control OpenAI's data retention policies
- **To Request Deletion**: Contact OpenAI directly at https://platform.openai.com/docs/api-usage/data-policies
- **Note**: We only send data to OpenAI when you actively use features like workout plan generation or form analysis. We do not store your data on OpenAI's servers ourselves.
